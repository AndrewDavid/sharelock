About

Our story is simple. We created Sharelock because we wanted a way to share secret data securely. We’re constantly exchanging bits of confidential information and, because we take security very seriously, we didn’t feel comfortable sharing that information through normal channels like email or chat.

So, Sharelock was born. It provides you with a simple and secure way to share secrets with people you know. It’s easy. You enter a secret and a list of people that should be allowed to access that secret. Sharelock encrypts and signs this information and sends you back a URL.

You send that URL out any way you’d like - publicly or privately - the channel does not need to be secure, as the Sharelock itself is already encrypted. Your recipient clicks on the URL and is required to authenticate using Auth0. Sharelock checks to see if the authenticated recipient is on the list of people you intended to share your secret with. If everything checks out, the data is decrypted and your recipient receives the secret!

We hope you will use and enjoy Sharelock as much as we do. When you have a moment, stop by Auth0 and set up a free account so you can forget about all of your authentication, authorization and single sign on worries.
